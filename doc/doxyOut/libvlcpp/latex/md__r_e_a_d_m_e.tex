\href{https://code.videolan.org/videolan/libvlcpp}{\texttt{ libvlcpp}} is a header-\/only, C++ bindings for the libvlc crossplatform multimedia A\+PI. It is an official binding over the Video\+L\+AN libvlc library and can be used on many platforms.\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysection{Documentation}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}
Since libvlcpp is a close wrapper over libvlc, your documentation needs will be best served by either checking the libvlcpp source code, or the \href{https://www.videolan.org/developers/vlc/doc/doxygen/html/group__libvlc.html}{\texttt{ libvlc documentation}}.\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysection{Issues}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}
libvlcpp tickets are located on our \href{https://code.videolan.org/videolan/libvlcpp/-/issues}{\texttt{ Git\+Lab}}.\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md3}{}\doxysection{Contribution}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md3}
Contribution are always welcome!

Feel free to open a merge request on our \href{https://code.videolan.org/videolan/libvlcpp/-/merge_requests}{\texttt{ Git\+Lab}}.\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md4}{}\doxysection{Examples}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md4}
